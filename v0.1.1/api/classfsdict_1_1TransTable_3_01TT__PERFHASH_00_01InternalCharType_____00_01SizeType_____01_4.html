template<typename InternalCharType__ , typename SizeType__ >
typedef InternalCharType__ fsdict::TransTable< TT_PERFHASH, InternalCharType__, SizeType__ >::InternalChar_t

This is the datatype used for storing transition labels in the TransTable.

Usually it is an unsigned type of 1-4 bytes (actually it's not really tested with anything but 2 bytes)

template<typename InternalCharType__ , typename SizeType__ >
typedef SizeType__ fsdict::TransTable< TT_PERFHASH, InternalCharType__, SizeType__ >::InternalSize_t

This is the type used for offsets in the sparse table.

Often it makes sense to use a 4byte type also on a 64bit machine to get portable binary files and/or to get a smaller representation
